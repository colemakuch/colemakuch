Bug Jazz

Bug Jazz is a collective 100% improvised recording/performing concept that eschews tonality to focus solely on vibes by blending aspects of rock guitar music with ambient textures and dance rhythms. Bug jazz strives for an aesthetic that's 40% spiritual free jazz and 30% Andrew WK 'the only thing better than a guitar solo is a guitar solo played on a guitar shaped like a pizza!'
