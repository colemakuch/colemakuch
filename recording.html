I help bands make records that capture the energy of performance in space while still retaining the polish of "real music". It was a big revalation to me that 'DIY' music can also sound great!
I can record:

  • 24 channels of digital audio through high-quality preamps and converters
  • 16 channels of analog audio onto half-inch reel-to-reel tape
  • 8 channels of analog audio onto cassette tape

I prefer working in basements/garages/living rooms/other cool spaces, but am also experienced in professional studios.

Contact me to discuss a project.
